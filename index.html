You will Never miss the Bus again

Cyprus Science University launch a brand new bus location tracking app made by it's own student

Bus Tracking App

The CSU slogan being “You First”, we commited to provide the best experience ever to our student in all aspects. And you are a student one of the situation might sound familiar to you:

  • you wait long time in the bus stop not knowing when the bus will come
  • you missed the university bus
  • you waited but the bus didn’t came because the schedule was changed and you didn’t know
  • the bus was late maybe trapped in a traffic jam, but you left the bus stop thinking you missed it.
Cyprus Science University comes with a solution to those problems, A Bus Tracking App designed by its own student.

App features

  • Real time tracking of the University bus, to let you where is the bus all the time
  • Personalizable notification system, to warn you when the bus is about to leave or is near you bus stop
  • Two language implemented: English and Turkish

How does it work

    Three simple steps

  • Download the app
  • Authentify yourself as CSU student
  • Enjoy the app

Download the Bus Tracking App

CSU student verification

After you dowloaded and installed the app you will reach this screen. you have to verify your status of Csu student,

  • Type your csu student email adress ( the one you use to connect on teams)
  • Then click on the link in orange.

You will be redirected to Microsoft outlook login page.
Then you simply have to login to with your teams login informations.

After you rich your inbox, find the email that says “Verification of you CSU account” and click on the verification link in it.

I you can’t find it kindly check on you spams or unwanted mail, it will surely be there.

Once your account has been verified, everything is Set !

Track the bus location

Once you are verified as CSU student, you can enjoy the app freely. you can track the bus in real time, configure your schedule to get notification when it is time to take the bus. The bus is no more an excuse to being late
