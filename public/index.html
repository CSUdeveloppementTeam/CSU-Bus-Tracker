Bus Tracking App

The CAU slogan being “Towards_A_Bright_Future”, the University is commited to provide the best experience ever to its student in all aspects. If you are a student one of the situations below might sound familiar to you:

  • you waited a long time in the bus stop not knowing when the bus will come
  • you missed the university bus
  • you waited but the bus didn’t came because the schedule was changed and you didn’t know
  • the bus was late maybe trapped in a traffic jam, but you left the bus stop thinking you missed it.

Cyprus Aydin University comes with a solution to those problems, A Bus Tracking App designed by its own students.

Features of the app

  • Real-time tracking of the University bus, to let you where is the bus all the time
  • Personalizable notification system, to warn you when the bus is about to leave or is near you bus stop
  • Two languages implemented: English and Turkish

Download the Bus Tracking App

The CAU Bus Tracking app is compatible with Android phone only for the moment. You can download it directly by scanning the Qr code here or anywhere in the campus.
